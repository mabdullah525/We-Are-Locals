Unmatchable Services are Just Around the Corner

We R Local is a mobile app that connects users with service-based businesses in their community such as restaurants, tour agencies, event organizers, and more.

Connect and Interact

As a social network, users and businesses can chat, create groups, and upload posts.

Virtual Business Card

List information about business operating hours, services, contact info, etc.

Map

Users can search for businesses in a specified area via the map feature.

eEcommerce

Users can purchase and make appointments directly through the app.

Why We Chose We R Locals

We R Locals is dedicated to connecting people with gems within their community. Users can explore local businesses that are well-rated, and be able to shop locally for things they need.

By creating a social platform of service-based businesses, these SMEs are empowered to market their products in a space where they can engage and advertise their offerings. They can also start conversations with their target market to get insight on what customers really want.

The app caters to the satisfaction rate of all users, making it obligatory for businesses to correct mistakes and uphold quality standards

Be a Part of Local Prosperity

Everything about We R Locals prioritizes the customer experience and the app’s design is no exception. With easy-to-use features, and intuitive icons anyone can use the We R Locals app to find businesses near them.

Announce Promotions

Market

Feedback
Once an unsatisfactory review is uploaded on We R Local, the business has a window of time to rectify the error and complete satisfaction rate.
